Common Queries

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the qualifications of the teaching staff?

All core subjects (Physics, Chemistry, Maths, Biology) are handled by highly qualified professionals, including B.Tech graduates from premier institutes (like IITs/NITs) and PhD experts, holding at least 8-12 years of coaching experience.

How are student doubts addressed outside the classes?

We schedule dedicated 1-on-1 doubt solving classes daily. Students can sit down directly with subject faculty after classes to work out complicated textbook or assignment problems.

Do you offer boarding/hostel counseling support?

Yes, we provide career counseling, support, and list recommendation of hostels/boarding options for outstation students who travel to Nagpur-Kamptee for study.

What is the standard batch strength at Taj Gurukul?

To maintain high-quality individual feedback, our maximum batch size is capped strictly at 30 students. This makes regular assessment and individual query feedback viable.
